Why convert WebP to JPG?

Some apps, email clients, marketplaces, and older websites still don't accept WebP. Converting WebP to JPG gives you a file that works everywhere, at a small, shareable size.

How to convert WebP to JPG

  • Drop your WebP into the box above (or click to browse). PNG, JPG, WebP, GIF and BMP all work as the source.
  • Pick a quality — 92% is a good balance of size and clarity. Then click Convert to JPG.
  • Check the before/after preview and the new file size, then hit Download JPG.

Frequently asked questions

What about transparency and quality?

JPG does not support transparency, so any transparent areas in the WebP are filled with white. Use the quality slider to control the trade-off between size and detail.

Is it really free and private?

Yes. This converter is 100% client-side: your image is read and converted on an HTML <canvas> right in your browser. Nothing is uploaded, there is no account, and no image ever leaves your device.

Can I convert several files at once?

This page handles one image at a time. Batch conversion is on the way — for now, convert and download each one, or use the main image tools page.
