Privacy Policy

ImageTools is built around a simple idea: your images are yours. This page explains exactly what happens to your files, what data we collect, and how cookies and advertising work on this site.

Your images never leave your device

Every tool on ImageTools — resizing, compressing, converting, removing EXIF data, and building PDFs — runs entirely in your web browser using standard browser technology (the Canvas API and WebAssembly). Your images are never uploaded to any server. There is no account, no cloud storage, and no copy of your file kept anywhere. Once you close the tab, nothing remains.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how many people visit the site and which tools are popular. This collects anonymous, aggregated information such as page views, approximate location (country/region), browser type, and device category. It does not include the contents of your images. This information helps us decide which tools and guides to build next.

Cookies and Advertising

This site uses cookies to improve your experience. We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to show ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

Third-party services

Google AdSense and Google Analytics are operated by Google LLC. Their use of data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this and other websites.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. Continued use of the site after a change means you accept the revised policy.
