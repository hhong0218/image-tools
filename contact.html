ImageTools is a small, free project. We read every message, though we may not be able to reply to all of them. Helpful things to include:

  • Feedback or feature requests — tell us which tool you use and what would make it better, or what tool is missing.
  • Bug reports — let us know the tool, your browser and operating system, and what went wrong. (Please don't send us your images — every tool runs on your own device, so we can't see them anyway.)
  • Privacy questions — see our Privacy Policy first; if it doesn't answer your question, email us.

For how we handle data, see our Privacy Policy. For the rules that govern use of the site, see our Terms of Service. To learn more about the project, visit the About page.
